Slo-mo Mod
==========

Enable Slo-mo capture in unsupported iOS 7+ devices and customize it.

Compatibility
==========

DEVICE (maximum FPS)

- iPhone 6s/6s plus (240)
- iPhone 6/6 plus (240)
- iPhone 5s/6 (120)
- iPhone 5/5c (60)
- iPod touch 5G (60)
- iPad Air 2/3 (120)
- iPad Air (60)
- iPad mini 3 (120)
- iPad mini 1G/2G (60)
- iPhone 4/4s (30)
- iPad 2/3/4 (30)
